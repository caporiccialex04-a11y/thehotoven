From scratch, always

A rural Oxford bakery with a barn-door welcome

Esther Scholten has been baking since she was eight. What began as roadside pound cakes grew into a food truck in Norwich, and now a certified kitchen inside a red barn storefront on Culloden Line.

Ingredients are sourced close to home — berries, fruit, and meats from neighbouring Oxford County farms — so every bite tastes like the season you’re in.

“Seasonal baked goods made completely from scratch. Enjoy the rich flavours in these delicious home-made treats.”
